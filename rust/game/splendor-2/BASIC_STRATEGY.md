# Splendor - Basic Strategy

Hard rules to avoid obviously bad moves.

## Taking tokens

- Only take gems you need for a card you can buy soon. Taking gems that don't progress toward a purchase wastes a turn.
- You cannot take Gold with the `take` action - Gold only comes from reserving. Don't try to take it.
- Taking 2 of the same gem requires 4+ of that gem in the bank. If fewer remain, you must take 3 different gems instead.
- Avoid taking tokens that push you over 10 total unless you can spend them next turn - anything over 10 must be discarded back.

## Buying cards

- A card's cost is reduced by your permanent bonuses before tokens are spent, and Gold covers any leftover shortfall automatically. Make sure you actually can afford a card before committing the turn to it.
- Don't burn scarce Gold to buy a 0-prestige level 1 card when you could save it for a card that needs the wildcard. There are only 5 Gold in the whole game.
- Prefer buying a card whose bonus colour you still need over an equally priced card whose bonus you already have plenty of.

## Reserving

- Never let your reserve sit full (3 cards) without buying from it - a full reserve blocks further reserving and the free Gold that comes with it.
- Only reserve a card you intend to buy. Reserving uses your whole turn for one card and (at best) one Gold.
- Reserve a card to grab a Gold when you need one, but only if the card is at least marginally useful to you.

## Nobles

- Nobles are free 3 prestige - don't ignore one you're close to affording. Check the nobles row before deciding which bonus colours to chase.
- Noble costs are paid in permanent card bonuses only, never tokens. The only way to attract a noble is to buy cards of the matching colours.
- When two or more nobles are affordable you must pick one with `visit`; the choice is yours, so pick before the turn stalls.

## Discarding

- When forced to discard down to 10, dump the gems least useful to your current plan - never a gem you need for a buy you're about to make.
- Gold counts toward the 10-token limit too. Don't hoard Gold you won't spend.
