# Red7 - Basic Strategy

Hard rules to avoid obviously bad moves.

## Staying alive

- Never end your turn without being the leader. If you are not winning under the current rule, you must either play a card to your palette to become the leader, or discard a card to change the rule to one you are winning under.
- If you cannot become the leader, do not play "done" - you will be eliminated.
- Before playing "done", verify you are the leader under the current rule.

## Playing cards

- You may only play one card to your palette per turn. Choose the card that makes you the leader or strengthens your lead.
- Do not play a card that does not make you the leader unless you also plan to discard to change the rule.

## Discarding (changing the rule)

- You may only discard if doing so makes you the leader under the new rule. The game enforces this.
- Discarding a card with rank higher than your palette size lets you draw a card. Prefer discarding high-rank cards when you need to refill your hand.
- Do not discard a card that gives your opponent an easy rule to win under.

## General

- Keep track of how many cards you have. Running out of cards at the start of your turn means immediate elimination.
- In later rounds, the deck may not have enough cards for a full deal. The game ends if the deck has fewer cards than players times 8.
