# 7 Wonders

Players develop their civilization over 3 ages, drafting cards simultaneously
each turn. Build structures, trade with neighbors, and construct your wonder
to accumulate the most victory points.

## Setup

Each player is assigned a random city with a wonder board and starting
resource. Players begin with 3 coins.

## Turn Structure

Each age, players are dealt 7 cards. Each turn, all players simultaneously
choose one action:

- **build N** - Build card N from your hand, paying its cost. You may buy
  resources from neighbors (2 coins each, or 1 with trade discounts).
- **free N** - Build card N for free using a wonder ability (Olympia).
- **wonder N** - Discard card N face-down under your wonder and build the
  next wonder stage, paying its cost.
- **discard N** - Discard card N from your hand and gain 3 coins.

After all players act, hands pass to the next player (left in odd ages,
right in even ages). This repeats until 1 card remains, which is
automatically discarded unless you have the PlayFinalCard ability.

## End of Age

After each age, military conflicts are resolved. Compare your total shield
strength with your right neighbor. The winner gains victory tokens (1/3/5
for ages 1/2/3), the loser gains a defeat token.

## Scoring

At game end, victory points come from:

- Victory tokens minus defeat tokens
- Coins (1 VP per 3 coins)
- Card VP values
- Science (sets of symbols: count^2 per type + 7 per complete set)
- Guild and commercial bonuses
- Wonder stages

The player with the most VP wins. Ties broken by coins.
