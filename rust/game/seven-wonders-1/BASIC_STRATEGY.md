# Seven Wonders - Basic Strategy

Hard rules to avoid obviously bad moves.

## Card selection

- You must choose one card per hand: build it, build a wonder stage (discarding the card), or discard it for 3 coins.
- Do not discard a card you can build for free (via prerequisites). Free builds are pure value.
- Do not build a card you cannot afford. Check your resources and coins, and whether neighbors can supply what you lack (at a trade cost).
- You cannot build a card if you already have one with the same name.

## Resource management

- Your city provides one starting resource. Resource cards (brown/grey) provide additional goods.
- Trading from neighbors costs 2 coins per good (1 with a trading post discount). Factor this into affordability.
- Do not ignore resource cards. Without them, you cannot build advanced cards in later ages.

## Wonder building

- Building a wonder stage requires paying its cost and discarding a card from your hand. The discarded card is lost.
- Wonder stages give powerful effects. Prioritize them when you have a card you would otherwise discard.
- You can only build wonder stages in order (stage 1 before stage 2, etc.).

## Military

- Military conflict happens at the end of each age. Compare your military strength with each neighbor.
- Winning gives victory tokens (1/3/5 for ages 1/2/3). Losing gives 1 defeat token (-1 VP).
- Do not ignore military completely. Having 0 strength guarantees defeat tokens every age.

## General

- Discarding gives 3 coins. This is a valid play when you have no good options and need coins.
- The last card of each age is automatically discarded (unless you have a PlayFinalCard effect). Plan accordingly.
- Hands pass left in ages 1 and 3, right in age 2. Track what you pass and what you receive.
