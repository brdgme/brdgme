# Basic Strategy

## Placement
- Don't place ships adjacent to each other - it makes them easier to find with area denial
- Place ships spread across the board, not clustered in one region
- Don't place all ships in predictable patterns (all edges, all corners, all horizontal)
- Mix orientations - use both horizontal and vertical placements

## Shooting
- Don't shoot at cells you've already shot at
- When you get a hit, shoot adjacent cells (up, down, left, right) before shooting randomly
- Don't ignore hits - follow up on them systematically until the ship is sunk
- Once a ship is sunk, stop shooting around it and resume searching elsewhere
