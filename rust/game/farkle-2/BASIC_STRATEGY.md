# Farkle Basic Strategy

Hard rules to avoid throwing away points:

- Never bank zero points. You must score at least one combination before you can call done.
- Always take at least one scoring die before deciding whether to continue your turn.
- Do not roll a single die when you have 300+ points in the turn. The farkle risk is not worth it.
- Do not roll all six dice when you already have a comfortable turn score unless you are behind.
- Do not risk a farkle on one or two dice when you have a good turn score banked.
- Know when to stop. Greed causes farkles. Banking 400-500 per turn wins games.
- Grab single 1s and 5s before re-rolling. They turn otherwise dead rolls into points.
