# Zombie Dice - Basic Strategy

Hard rules to avoid obviously bad moves.

## When to keep

- Always keep if you have 2 shotguns. One more shotgun busts you and you lose all brains this turn.
- Keep if you have 1 shotgun and the cup is mostly red dice. Red dice have a 50% shotgun rate.
- Keep if you have banked enough to reach or exceed 13 brains. Winning is better than pushing for more.
- Do not roll with 2 shotguns unless you have 0 brains this turn and nothing to lose.

## When to roll

- Roll when you have 0 shotguns and the cup has green or yellow dice available.
- Roll when you have 0-1 brains this turn and few shotguns. The expected value of another roll is positive.
- Roll when you are behind and need to catch up. Conservative play does not win from behind.

## Dice awareness

- Green dice: 50% brain, 33% footprints, 17% shotgun. Safe to roll.
- Yellow dice: 33% brain, 33% footprints, 33% shotgun. Moderate risk.
- Red dice: 17% brain, 33% footprints, 50% shotgun. Avoid rolling red dice if possible.
- Count the dice in the cup. If only red dice remain, even 1 shotgun is dangerous.

## Cup management

- When the cup runs dry, all your kept dice (including shotguns) go back in. A long turn increases the chance of drawing red dice on the refill.
- Footprint dice are re-rolled automatically. You do not choose which dice to re-roll; footprints always continue.
