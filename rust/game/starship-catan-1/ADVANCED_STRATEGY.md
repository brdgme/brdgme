# Starship Catan - Advanced Strategy

Higher-level strategic considerations.

## Module upgrade path

- Modules can be upgraded to level 2 (costs 1 Ore + 1 Carbon + N Food where N is the new level). Level 2 modules give a victory point each.
- Command 2 gives 4 flight actions per turn, enabling deep exploration.
- Logistics 2 raises your goods limit to 4, preventing resource waste.
- Science 2 produces on dice 1-3 (always). Trade 2 produces on dice 1-3 (always).
- You cannot upgrade to level 2 if your opponent already has level 2 in that module. Race for key upgrades.

## Sector management

- Sectors have 10 cards each. Track which sectors are running low. A depleted sector means shorter flights.
- The Sensor module lets you peek at the top cards before committing. Use this to avoid wasted flights.
- Different sectors have different card distributions. Learn which sectors favor colonies vs trade vs pirates.

## Economy

- Astro (money) is the universal currency. Trading posts generate income by letting you sell resources at favorable prices.
- Build trading posts on cards with high sell prices for resources you produce abundantly.
- The Trade module's "take" action (2 Astro to steal a resource) is efficient when you need a specific resource and your opponent has it.

## Ship management

- You can hold at most 2 ships total. Building a third is impossible until you spend one.
- Colony ships found colonies (permanent VP + production). Trade ships found trading posts (permanent trade income).
- Balance between colonies (production) and trading posts (income) based on your strategy.

## Pirate strategy

- Pirates with high strength (3+) are dangerous without cannons. Build cannons before engaging them.
- Some pirates destroy modules on a loss. If you have a level 2 module, the risk is higher.
- Defeated pirates contribute to Hero of the People (most medals). If you are ahead on medals, keep fighting.

## Adventure cards

- Adventures require specific resources to complete. Read the requirements before committing a flight action.
- Some adventures give resources back, some give VP, some give special abilities. Prioritize VP adventures.
- Adventure planets appear in specific sectors. Plan your flights around available adventures.

## End-game

- At 7+ VP, every action matters. Calculate whether you or your opponent will hit 10 first.
- Modules at level 2 give 1 VP each. Upgrading can be the fastest path to 10.
- Friend/Hero of the People cards give 1 VP each. Maintain your lead on diplomat points/medals if you hold them.
