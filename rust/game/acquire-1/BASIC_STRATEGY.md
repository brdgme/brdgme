# Acquire Basic Strategy

## Tile placement
- Never place a tile adjacent to two safe corporations - it is illegal and wastes your turn planning.
- Do not place a tile that founds a corporation unless you can afford at least one share in it, or you specifically want the free founder share.
- Avoid placing tiles that grow an opponent's majority corporation unless you also hold shares there.
- If you hold tiles that are permanently unplayable (adjacent to two safe corps), they will be auto-discarded - plan around losing them.

## Buying shares
- Always buy at least one share of a corporation you just founded - you already got a free founder share, so buying more secures your majority cheaply.
- Never buy zero shares when you have cash and an active corporation exists. Skipping buys wastes your only growth opportunity each turn.
- Do not buy shares in a corporation you cannot affect. With only 3 buys per turn, focus on corporations where you can reach or defend majority.
- Do not spend your last cash on shares. Running out of money means you cannot buy into future opportunities or survive a dry spell.

## Mergers
- Never merge a larger corporation into a smaller one when you are the majority holder of the larger. You destroy your own bonus.
- When you are the mergemaker and can choose the survivor, pick the corporation where you hold the most shares or where the surviving price is highest.
- Do not place a tile that triggers a merger where an opponent holds majority in the acquired corporation and you hold nothing - you are handing them free money.
- After a merger, prefer trading 2-for-1 into the survivor over selling, unless you urgently need cash or the survivor has no shares left in the bank.

## Game end
- Track the tile count. The game ends when the draw bag empties - do not get caught holding worthless shares you planned to grow.
- Do not trigger the end of the game while opponents hold large positions in corporations about to pay bonuses, unless your own position is stronger.
- When all corporations are safe, the game can end at any time - convert your share positions into cash-ready bonuses immediately.
