# Modern Art - Basic Strategy

Hard rules to avoid obviously bad moves.

## Bidding

- Never bid more than the artist's current total value for a single card. At round end you are paid the artist's total value for each card you hold, so a card is worth at most that much to you.
- Never bid money you cannot afford to lose. If you spend down to near $0 you cannot buy anything for the rest of the round.
- In an Open auction, stop raising once the price passes the card's value to you. Do not bid up out of pride and overpay.
- You cannot bid or buy above your current money. Check your cash before committing.

## Playing cards (as auctioneer)

- Do not play the 5th card of an artist unless ending the round helps you. The 5th card is not sold and earns you nothing; it ends the round immediately.
- If you win your own auction you pay the bank, so it is a net cost (cash for a card). Only let that happen when the card is worth more to you than the price.
- Selling to another player is pure profit (their money comes to you). Prefer to set things up so others buy from you.

## Fixed Price

- Do not set a price below a card's value if you would be unhappy to lose it - an opponent can buy it cheaply.
- Do not set a price so high that nobody buys and you are forced to buy it yourself at that price.

## Double auctions

- Only add a second card to a Double auction if you are willing to sell both together; the added card goes to the winner for the single payment.
- Do not add a card of an artist you are trying to collect for yourself.

## Sealed auctions

- Do not pass (bid $0) on a card you actually want - you get only one secret bid.
- Do not bid more than the card's value. There is no rival bidder pushing the price; you simply pay exactly what you bid.

## Once Around

- You get exactly one chance to bid. If the card is worth having, bid before your turn passes.

## General

- Only cards you purchase pay out at scoring. Starting auctions you never win spends your turn for nothing.
- Track every player's purchases - they are public - so you know which artists are close to the top three.
