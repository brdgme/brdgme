# For Sale - Basic Strategy

- Don't overpay for low-value properties early in the buying phase
- Don't drop out of an auction with a high bid just to save 1 chip
- Don't play your highest property card first in the selling phase
- Don't bid more than a property's value in chips (you need chips for later auctions)
- Don't ignore what properties others have - their hand affects your selling strategy
- Save some chips for the later, more valuable property auctions
