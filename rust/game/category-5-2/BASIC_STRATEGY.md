# Category 5 Basic Strategy

- Don't play a card that will be the 6th in a row unless you have no choice - you take the row and all its bullheads.
- When forced to take a row, take the one with the fewest bullheads.
- Don't play a card just below a row's last card if the row already has 5 cards - you will take that row.
- Play high cards early when rows are short to avoid being stuck with them later.
- Don't waste low cards early - you'll need them when rows fill up and you need a safe play.
- Track which cards have been played to predict where your card will land.
