# Sushi Go - Advanced Strategy

Higher-level strategic considerations.

## Drafting awareness

- You are not just choosing what to take; you are choosing what to pass. Denying an opponent a card they need is as valuable as taking a card you need.
- Track what you pass. If you pass sashimi to the left, the player to your left may complete a set. Consider keeping cards that complete opponent combos.
- In later hands (fewer cards remaining), the value of completing a combo increases because there are fewer opportunities to draw what you need.

## Combo prioritization

- Sashimi (3 for 10) and tempura (2 for 5) are all-or-nothing. If you have 2 sashimi and no realistic path to a third, pivot to something else.
- Dumplings are the most consistent scorer. Even 3 dumplings for 6 points is solid. They do not require specific pairings.
- Wasabi + squid nigiri = 9 points, the highest single-combo value. Wasabi + salmon = 6, wasabi + egg = 3. Prioritize wasabi when high nigiri are available.

## Maki strategy

- Maki is a relative scoring mechanic. You do not need many maki; you need more than everyone else. In a 2-player game, you only need to beat one opponent.
- If no one else is taking maki, even a few maki rolls can win the 6-point bonus cheaply.
- Do not over-invest in maki if you are already winning the maki race. The marginal maki card beyond your lead is worth 0 points.

## Pudding endgame

- Pudding is scored only at the end of round 3. In rounds 1 and 2, pudding is a tempo loss (you give up a scoring card for a delayed reward).
- In 3+ player games, the fewest puddings gets -6. If you are at 0 puddings in round 3, taking one pudding can save you 6 points.
- In 2-player games, there is no pudding penalty, only the +6 bonus. Pudding is less urgent.

## Round management

- Round 1 passes left, round 2 passes right, round 3 passes left. The passing direction affects which neighbor you are drafting against.
- Build flexible hands early. Commit to specific combos in rounds 2 and 3 when you have more information about what is available.
